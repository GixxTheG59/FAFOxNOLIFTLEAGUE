COMMUNITY RULES

To ensure everyone has the best experience, please follow these simple but important rules.

IN-GAME RULES

  1. No intentional crashing or griefing other players
  2. Respect all players regardless of skill level
  3. No offensive liveries or driver names
  4. Follow admins' instructions at all times
  5. No cheating or exploiting game mechanics
  6. Keep chat clean and friendly
  7. No spamming or excessive mic usage
  8. Respect track limits and driving etiquette

DISCORD RULES

  1. No NSFW or offensive content
  2. Keep discussions relevant to channels
  3. No spamming or self-promotion
  4. Use English in main channels
  5. Respect all members and staff
  6. Follow Discord's Terms of Service
  7. No drama or toxic behavior
  8. Keep political/religious discussions out

CONSEQUENCES

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from our community depending on severity. We want to maintain a positive environment for all performance enthusiasts.
